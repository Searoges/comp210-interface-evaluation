Recommendations based on survey

  • Tutorial level with easy to follow directions much like Squishy The Suicidal Pig where signs directly tell the player what to do
  • More clear feedback when things occur within the game potentially some form of tactile feedback
  • Some form of health bar or level progress bar on the UI

Recommendations based on expert heuristic analysis

  • Documentation layed out by language for easy parsing
  • Some form of more evident or tactile feedback
  • Tutorial level with easy to follow directions much like Squishy The Suicidal Pig where signs directly tell the player what to do
  • Icons on the UI to make it more obvious to players
  • Within tutorial clearly define the purpose of rings and power ups
